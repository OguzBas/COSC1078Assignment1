Project Idea

My ideal It project is about something that brings software and daily life needs together. The project is about commanding to electrical devices and controlling them.People have electrical devices such as oven, air-conditioner, Tv, computer, fridge and so on. We always need to control them by touching their buttons. When we are close to these devices, we can control them easily. What if we are far from these devices and we need to control them. Then, we need to change our location. This means we lose time, energy and time. This project will save us energy, time and money. By having a online control over a device, we would be able to monitor and control them. And this project should interest very broad range of people no matter what their job is, no matter what their age is and no matter where they live, because everyone deals with electrical devices in some way.
